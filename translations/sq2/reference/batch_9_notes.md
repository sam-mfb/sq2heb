## Key Translation Decisions

### 1. Cliff Dweller Dialogue (Logic 29)

**Context:** The pink cliff dwellers speak to Roger after he saves one of them.

**Message 4:** "Welcome to our canyon. You look to be from out of town."
- **Translation:** "ברוך הבא לקניון שלנו. אתה נראה כמו מחוץ לעיר."
- **Note:** Simple, friendly greeting maintaining the casual tone

**Message 5:** "On behalf of all of us, I would like to thank you for saving our compadre from the hunters."
- **Translation:** "בשם כולנו, אני רוצה להודות לך על שהצלת את החבר שלנו מהציידים."
- **Note:** "compadre" → "החבר שלנו" (our friend) - keeping it casual and friendly

**Message 6:** Long message about staying/leaving
- **Translation:** Maintained formal-yet-friendly tone with "אתה מוזמן" (you're welcome)
- **Note:** "being of your size" → "יצור בגודל שלך" - polite way of saying Roger is too big for normal exits

**Message 7:** "When you leave, however, we must seal the exit..."
- **Translation:** "כשתצא, עם זאת, אנחנו חייבים לאטום..."
- **Note:** Preserved the formal "however" with "עם זאת"

### 2. Sarcastic Narrator Commentary (Logic 29)

**Message 9:** "Besides, you can barely walk and wink at the same time."
- **Translation:** "חוץ מזה, אתה בקושי יכול ללכת ולקרוץ בעין באותו הזמן."
- **Note:** Classic SQ insult to Roger's coordination - kept direct and sarcastic

**Message 26:** "How typical. The simple mind resorting to violence in time of frustration."
- **Translation:** "כמה טיפוסי. המוח הפשוט נוקט באלימות בשעת תסכול."
- **Note:** Narrator mocking player for trying violence - maintained condescending tone

**Message 28:** "I wouldn't be sitting back on my keister if I were you."
- **Translation:** "הייתי לא יושב על התחת אם הייתי אתה."
- **Note:** "keister" (slang for butt) → "התחת" - using direct Hebrew slang

### 3. Action Sequence - Shuttle Bay Kidnapping (Logic 5)

**Context:** Roger enters shuttle, gets beaten up, kidnapped. Key plot moment.

**Message 2:** "There are two extremely ugly suckers walking toward you."
- **Translation:** "יש שני פראיירים מכוערים במיוחד שצועדים לקראתך."
- **Note:** "suckers" → "פראיירים" (suckers/marks) - Israeli slang fits perfectly here

**Message 3:** "Hey! What the (your favorite expletive here)..."
- **Translation:** "היי! מה ל(השמט את הביטוי האהוב עליך כאן)..."
- **Note:** Meta-joke about censoring swearing - kept the parenthetical instruction

**Messages 4-7:** Sound effects (POW!! THWACK!! BINCK!! THUD!!!)
- **Translations:** באם!! טראק!! בינק!! דוף!!!
- **Note:** Transliterated comic book sound effects into Hebrew phonetics
  - POW → באם (ba'am - explosion sound)
  - THWACK → טראק (trak - hitting sound)
  - BINCK → בינק (bink - kept close to original)
  - THUD → דוף (dof - thud/thump sound)

**Message 8:** "Your protest is cut short as two interstellar ruffians..."
- **Translation:** "המחאה שלך נקטעת כששני בריונים בין-כוכביים..."
- **Note:** "ruffians" → "בריונים" (thugs/bullies) - appropriate term

**Message 11-12:** Time passage and awakening

## Technical Notes

### Placeholder Usage:

- **%m** references preserved (message concatenation)
  - Example: "%m8 Besides, you can barely..." → "%m8 חוץ מזה..."
  - Maintains game's message combining system

### Sound Effects Strategy:

Created Hebrew phonetic equivalents for comic book sounds:
- **POW** → **באם** (ba'am)
- **THWACK** → **טראק** (trak)
- **BINCK** → **בינק** (bink)
- **THUD** → **דוף** (dof)

Rationale: Hebrew readers need to "hear" the sounds, so transliteration beats keeping English.

## Notable Challenges

### 1. Plunger Puzzle Description (66.15)

**Original:** "Using formerly uncharacteristic creativity, you apply the suction cup-like plunger to the smooth metal finish and hang on for dear life! Once a janitor, always a janitor!"

**Challenge:**
- Long, complex sentence
- "suction cup-like" modifier
- Idiom "hang on for dear life"
- Tagline about janitor identity

**Solution:**
- "תוך שימוש ביצירתיות לא אופיינית בעבר"
- "שואב פקקים דמוי כוס-היניקה" (plunger like-suction-cup)
- "נאחז לחיים" (gripping for life)
- "פעם שוטף, תמיד שוטף" (once janitor, always janitor)

### 2. Cliff Dweller Dialogue Tone

**Challenge:** Balancing friendly politeness with alien-ness

**Solution:**
- Used formal-but-warm Hebrew ("אתה מוזמן")
- Kept "החבר שלנו" instead of more formal options
- "יצור בגודל שלך" (being of your size) - polite distancing

### 3. Acid Death Description

**Challenge:** Graphic violence while maintaining game's dark humor

**Solution:**
- Didn't soften the description (true to SQ style)
- "אבן ספוג" for pumice (exact technical term)
- Preserved progression: displeasure → worse → organs → death
- Kept sarcastic "mercifully" → "ברחמים"
