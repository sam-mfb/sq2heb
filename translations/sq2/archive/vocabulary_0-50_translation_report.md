## Verb Form Strategy

Following translation instructions, **all verbs include multiple conjugations** to match natural player typing:

### Example: Word 2 (examine/look)
```
✅ Imperative: בדוק, בחן, הבט, הסתכל, ראה
✅ Infinitive: לבדוק, לבחון, להביט, להסתכל, לראות
✅ Future: תבדוק, תבחן, תביט, תסתכל, תראה
```

### Why Multiple Forms Matter:
1. **Natural Typing:** Players type what feels natural ("תסתכל על הדלת" vs "הסתכל על הדלת")
2. **Tense Variation:** Hebrew speakers naturally mix imperative and future tense in commands
3. **Synonym Coverage:** Multiple verbs for same action (examine = בדוק/בחן/התבונן)

**All 19 translated verbs follow this pattern.**
